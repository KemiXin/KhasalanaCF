About Mid-Autumn Festival

The Mid Autumn Festival, commonly known in China as the Moon Festival, is also one of the most important celebrations in Chinese Culture. Just like Spring Festival, families gather together to moon gaze and enjoy mooncakes.

Legend of Chang'e

There was a famous story in China where many families would share it to the younger folks, talking about the legend of Chang'e, The Moon Goddess.

If you're interested in reading the full story of the Moon Goddess, Chang'e. You can visit: China Highlights - Mid Autumn Festival

Traditions

The people celebrate by lighting lanterns, moon-gazing, worshipping the moon and offering sacrifices, folk performances and cultural shows.
